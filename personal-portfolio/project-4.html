Project 4

MAKE A REPAIR

This is a project I did for a previous course in which the topic was "make a repair". I had been going through a rough patch due to some personal issues and decided to center this project on me. I was reparing my self. Art has always been my emotional outlet and in doing this project I gave myself the time and space to heal and do what I love. It also sort of mimicked a stress-therapy coloring book that i basically made for myself.
